SCC Consultation Policies & Procedures


  • Prior to starting the form please have ready: your hypotheses and proposed analyses, vaiables (i.e. IV, DV, moderators, mediators, etc.), scale of your variables (i.e. continuous, categoical, ordinal, count), and structure of your data (i.e. repeated measures, pre- post-test, composite score vs individual indicators)

  • Upon completion of form, your responses will be reviewed by the student director. During this processess you may receive an email asking for additional information in order to assist you.

  • When the student director has all information, you will be assigned to a statistics consultant. Please allow for up to 1 week for assignment.

  • You will then need to contact the consultant to schedule your first meeting. Please allow up to 1 week for the first meeting with your consultant.

  • You are allotted 6 hours of total consultation; the first meeting will be up to 1 hour and is intended for the consultant to develop an understanding of your project and your specific needs. You will then have 5 hours of consultation after the initial meeting. This can be utilized at your, and your consultants discretion.

    • For Dissertations


    In order to maintain the acacdemic integrity of the dissertation process, we do not provide direct consultation for statistics software question. Our consultants are not allowed to review syntax, troubleshoot technical errors, or help write any code on your behalf. If you are seeking such support we recommend visiting the following websites:
  • stackoverflow for R & RStudio
  • statmodel for MPLUS
  • Laerd Statistics for SPSS
