Palo Alto University Statistics Consulting Center


Welcome to the Palo Alto University Statistics Consultation Center (or SCC). The SCC is run by Palo Alto University Ph.D. students who are supervised and advised by Tilman Schulte, Ph.D. The goal of the SCC is to provide PAU graduate students, post-docs, and faculty with statistical consultation services for research projects (i.e., dissertation, conference presentation, manuscript submissions). The SCC also sponsors workshops (1-2 per quarter) describing the application of nuanced or advanced statistical methods. The SCC does not provide tutoring for statistics coursework or preparation for comprehensive exams. Contact your research advisor, course instructor, or teaching assistant for information on statistics tutoring.
